Our Story


We strive to connect students to the right resources at the right time.


Our Mission


We believe that the overabundance of information, the siloed resources, and the state of UC Berkeley’s support system have inhibited students from achieving holistic wellness on campus.

Connect@Cal serves as UC Berkeley’s trusted resource hub, connecting students to the most relevant resources and opportunities with a simple form submission.

Our Vision


We hope to reshape the way students and resource providers on campus connect through identifying and addressing every resource gap in the system.

In doing so, we hope students can achieve holistic health and confidence needed to succeed at UC Berkeley.
