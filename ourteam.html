Core Team


Navigating college is one of the most difficult and daunting experiences for students. Discovering campus resources should be easier, and that's why I founded Connect@Cal. It was based on the notion that resources should not be hard to reach, and that every student must have a service to get connected to the right resources, at the right time.

- Johnny Nguyen, Founder of Connect@Cal
